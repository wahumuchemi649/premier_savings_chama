Saving rules

Members must save at least ksh 1000 per month

Missing a saving month incurs a fine of ksh 300

Members who join mid-year earn interest only from their join month onwards

Please note that if your payment is more than 10 days overdue ,the guarantor will become responsible for repaying the principal balance along with the interest
